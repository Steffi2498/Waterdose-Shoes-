ABOUT WATERDÖSE

During the pandemic, I had a desire to let out some pent up artistic energy. Through all the chaos, the idea of using some markers and paint that I had in my room to color some of my belongings was something that brought me joy. When some of my friends, family and surroundings caught wind, they suggested I make a business. This is that business. After SEVERAL years, I finally did it. This is the beginning of what I believe will be an extremely difficult, long and eventful journey. When looking for a name for this company, I originally thought of using some acronym or derivative of my actual name. But that just felt a little weird for me. One day, as I was thinking of names, I thought of the vine. More specifically, "WHAT ARE THOSE?!". And since it makes me laugh, it's the perfect name.
